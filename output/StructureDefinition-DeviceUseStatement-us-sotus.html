Resource Profile: DeviceUseStatement (SOTUS)

Official URL: http://hl7.org/fhir/us/sotus/StructureDefinition/DeviceUseStatement-us-sotus Version: 0.0.1
Standards status: Trial-use Maturity Level: 0 Computable Name: DeviceUseStatementUsSotus

This profile represents the constraints applied to the DeviceUseStatement resource by the Summary of the United States Implementation Guide (SOTUS), based on FHIR R4. A statement about a device used by or implanted on the patient is described in the patient summary as an instance of a DeviceUseStatement resource constrained by this profile.

This profile constrains the representation of a statement about a medical device used by or impanted on the patient, part of the "Medical Devices" section of the SOTUS Composition resource.

Usages:

  • Use this Profile: Bundle (SOTUS)
  • Refer to this Profile: Composition (SOTUS)
